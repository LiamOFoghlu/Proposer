{{ block title }} The Offer {{ endblock }} {{ block content }}

You have {{Constants.money}}, but you cannot keep all of it. You will be randomly matched with a receiver and must offer some of the money to them. You can offer them either 40% or 10%. The rest goes to you.


Types of receiver

There are two types of receiver: Choice, and No-choice. A Choice receiver may choose to reject your offer. If they reject it, you both get zero. A No-choice receiver must accept your offer.

There are an equal number of Choice and No-choice receivers, so there is a fifty-fifty chance you are matched with either. The receiver will not know whether you did the maths task.
